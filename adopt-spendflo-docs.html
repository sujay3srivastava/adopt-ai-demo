02 Business Objectives & Success Definition
💬
Replace Manual Dashboard Navigation with Conversational AI
Spendflo customers currently navigate complex multi-screen workflows to answer procurement questions (e.g., renewal timelines, spend by vendor). The co-pilot must enable natural language answers in under 10 seconds — "What's my Q2 renewal pipeline?" — within the product, without page navigation.
🔒
Full RBAC Compliance Across Multi-Tenant Architecture
Spendflo serves procurement, finance, and IT teams across the same organization with different data permissions. The co-pilot must respect existing role-based access controls with zero data leakage across roles or tenants. No rebuilds to their permission layer.
⚡
Drive Product Adoption — Co-pilot Usage Displaces Traditional Navigation
Success is measured by a sustained shift in how users interact with the product: co-pilot query volume should rise while traditional click-through navigation to the same data declines. Yash's benchmark: "We'll know we've nailed it when co-pilot replaces navigation for routine queries."
